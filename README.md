# 🧠 MINDFLUX – Portable EEG Monitoring System



---


## 💡 Our Solution – MINDFLUX

**MINDFLUX** is a **fully portable and affordable EEG-based wearable** that brings clinical-grade brain monitoring and mental health insights directly to users.

It’s designed to:
- Detect seizures, strokes, and stress levels in real-time.
- Provide cognitive wellness insights.
- Deliver early warnings and relaxation protocols automatically.

---

## ⚙️ Core Technology

### 🩺 Dual-Modality Detection System
- **EEG Electrodes (Scalp):** Continuous monitoring of brain signals.


### 🧩 Cognitive Wellness Features
- **Stress Analysis & Focus Training:** Quantifies stress and cognitive load.

---

## 🔬 Methodology

### **Hardware Components**
- 8 Dry EEG Electrodes  
- Instrumentation Amplifier (AD620)  
- Filters using OPA2277 (Noise Removal)  
- ESP32 (Signal Processing & Connectivity)

---

## 🧰 System Capabilities

### I. **Critical Health & Safety**
- Real-time **seizure and stroke** detection.  
- AI-driven **emergency alerts** to hospitals or family.  
-

### II. **Cognitive Wellness & Performance**
- Real-time **neurofeedback dashboard.**  
- Calculates **Focus Score** and **Stress Index.**  
- Tracks **sleep stages** and supports guided brainwave training.

### III. **Hardware Design**
- Lightweight wearable EEG cap/band.  
- Clinical-grade fidelity with ultra-low cost.  
- Suitable for home use with continuous monitoring.
